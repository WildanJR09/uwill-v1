uWill Accessibility Statement

Accessibility Commitment

We believe that learning should be available to everyone, which is why accessibility is an area of focus for us. Our teams are constantly working to improve the accessibility of our services in a variety of ways. Here are some examples of how we’re building a more accessible uWill:

  • Accesibility guidelines. We strive to follow the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) and use the Accessible Rich Internet Applications (ARIA) specification on the uWill website, mobile applications, and course videos.
  • Design updates. Accessibility is a core principle of our internal design system. Our engineers and designers work with accessibility consultants on design updates.
  • Compatibility testing. We test our website and mobile apps for compatibility with multiple screen readers and accessibility tools.
  • Ongoing research. We work with accessibility consultants to audit our services and provide recommendations to improve accessibility for everyone.

Accessibility Assistance

If you have any difficulty using or accessing uWIll, or any suggestions on how we can improve the accessibility of our services, please feel free to email us at accessibility@uwill.com. We look forward to hearing from you.
